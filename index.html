Who We Are

Two Birds One Stone is one of the best canteen cafes in South Yarra, VIC. It’s where you can have a delicious vegan breakfast with your favorite items. Whether you want to just have a cup of coffee or enjoy a delicious brunch, this French café has everything you would need to enjoy your café time. This uniquely equipped café serves the best coffee and best brunch. Our aromatic coffee will certainly make your day by making you fresh!
